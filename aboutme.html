Leisure

I enjoy having free time to spend on my hobbies such as sports, biking, hiking and playing video games. I enjoy going on long walks with my dog Ollie through trails, dogparks, or even just around the block. Living in the Niagara Region, I have access to the Niagara Falls, Niagara River, the Niagara escarpment and other amazing views that make going for walks so enjoyable. However, when I want to speed things up, I hop on my bike and hit the trails. This is a hobby I have gotten into recently since the sports that I used to play have stopped running. I will however take the time to throw a frisbee at any chance I get as I love playing ultimate frisbee and even played on the varsity team at Brock University. When I want to take things light, I enjoy playing video games to pass the time.

Professional Experiences

My professional experiences do not make up a very long list but I see them as very valuable for the direction I want to be heading when it comes to a career. I recently graducated from Brock University where I studied Geography and was first exposed to working with Geographic Information Systems (GIS). I developed a passion for it and have worked in the public and private sectors working with GIS and have enjoyed all of it. The experiences I had allowed me to work in an office and in the field, making it a dream job of mine as I love to get outdoors. I am currently continuing my education in GIS applications at Fleming College and I am excited to see where I go from here.
